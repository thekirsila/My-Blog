Hellooo Internet!
Hello you who has actually taken the time to visit my humble page. Thank you for that. I am a student living in the nordic countries and in the middle of college studies. I love dealing with computers and thought that making this page would be an awesome challenge for my programming skills and writing regularly would make the texts I write better. I have plans to add other stuff on this page, I might add coding projects, graphical designing, videos, but I guess only time will tell what will happen... Who knows what will happen? Who knows if I'll even keep this blog up for so long that I pass my finals? What I think I have decided is that if I ever make it to 400 monthly views I'll put ads on the page. Nothing too big and annoying but something to try to cover the costs of maintaining a server. Only time will tell...

Update 4th May 2016
Okay, here we are, I have got a couple of texts up, programmed the main scripts and made everything work... More or less. I also managed to make a partner. I also got advertisements up and running, let's see if they'll stay. They do slow down the page but also help with the costs of the server. Official launch should be in a couple of days.. Let's see if I'll get anyone interested.


Update 15th May 2016
Due to the fact that the life of a student is really seasonal and has moments where you really can't thinking about anything but school and moments when you have all the time in the world, I am testing out the "thought of the day". Which is not in any way daily, but hopefully somewhat more frequent than the (nearly) weekly updates. Let's see..
